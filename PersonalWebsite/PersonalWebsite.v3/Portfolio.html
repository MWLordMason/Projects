Four Against Darkness Webapp

Four against darkness is normally played using pen, paper, and a rulebook. This personal project of mine is currently in the works to help gameplay become simple, fast, and easy to simply log on and play. This webapp's sole purpose is to generate information such as random dice results, random pieces of content within the game that you'll stumble upon, and to help keep track of statistics such as your party of characters and their different stat values. This tool will not completely replace the pen and paper expereince, you will still get to draw the maps yourself and also fight monsters as well. This is a non-profit tool that I and many other who like the game could also use and enjoy the experience even more.
